Bake A Cake !

Indulge in the ultimate chocolate experience with our decadent chocolate cake. Rich, moist, and utterly irresistible, it's the perfect treat for any occasion
Heres what to do !

PREPARATION TIME

Preparation: 10 minutes
Baking: 40 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp chocolate powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup butter

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Add the flour,powdered sugar,baking powder and chocolate powder in a bowl and mix them well
  2. Add the eggs into a separate bowl
  3. Add melted butter and milk to it
  4. Stir everything well and add it to the dry ingredients
  5. Grease the baking tray with butter and add the batter to it
  6. Preheat the oven for 10 minutes
  7. Bake the cake for 40 minutes in the oven

The cake is ready to eat !!
